Maximize Your Time

Since 1971, Liberty’s mission as a university has been to provide a world-class educational experience with a solid Christian foundation.

We provide the same quality course offerings online as on our residential campus. As the world’s largest Christian university, we stand by you as you grow in your career and faith.

  • 100% online programs at associate, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral level.*
  • No standardized testing.
  • Transfer up to 90 credits into a bachelor’s degree program.
  • Up to 50% of your master’s degree can be transferred in to help you get the most out of your hard work and maximize the credit you previously earned.

* excludes licensure, certification, and some doctoral programs
